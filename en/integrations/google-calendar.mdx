### Integration with your Google Calendar account

[Create your chatbot here](https://doko-ai.com/en/sign-up)

<Steps>
  <Step>
    <h3>Go to Settings>Integrations in your chatbot portal and select Google Calendar</h3>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/boton-gc1.png" alt="Connect" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    <h3>Click on Connect</h3>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/boton-gc2.png" alt="Connect" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    <h3>Connect the Doko app with your Google account</h3>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/cuenta-gc1.png" alt="Connect" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    <h3>Grant the necessary permissions for Doko to access your Google account</h3>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/permisos1.png" alt="Permissions" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    <h3>Done! Your chatbot can now schedule appointments in your Google Calendar</h3>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/conectado-gc1.png" alt="Chat Bot" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
