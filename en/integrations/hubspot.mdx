### Integration with your Hubspot account

<Steps>
    <Step>
        <h3>Go to Settings>Integrations in your chatbot portal and select Hubspot</h3>
       <Frame>
  <img src="/images/integracion-hubspot.png" alt="Integrations" />
</Frame>
    </Step>
 <Step>
        <h3>Click on Connect</h3>
       <Frame>
  <img src="/images/boton-hubspot.png" alt="Connect" />
</Frame>
    </Step>
     <Step>
        <h3>Connect the Doko app with your Hubspot account</h3>
       <Frame>
  <img src="/images/hubspot-conect.png" alt="Connect" />
</Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step>
        <h3>Grant the necessary permissions for Doko to access your Hubspot account</h3>
       <Frame>
  <img src="/images/permisos-hubspot.png" alt="Permissions" />
 
</Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step>
        <h3>Done! Your chatbot can now save leads to your Hubspot account</h3>
       <Frame>
  <img src="/images/hubspot-done.png" alt="Chat Bot" />
</Frame>
    </Step>
</Steps>
