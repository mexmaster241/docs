### Prerequisites

Meta Business Account
- You need a Meta Business account to manage your professional Instagram account
Facebook Page
- An active Facebook page that you manage
Professional Instagram Account
- Your Instagram account must be converted to a Professional Account (Business or Creator)
- Connected to your Facebook page

<Note>If you need more information [Create a Meta Business account](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/898752960195806) </Note>
