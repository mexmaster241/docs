### Prerequisites

1. Create a Meta Business Portfolio
You need to have a Meta Business Portfolio to use the WhatsApp integration.

You can create a business portfolio at [Meta for Business](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/1710077379203657).

2. Get an exclusive phone number for this chatbot

You need to obtain a phone number that hasn't been used with WhatsApp Business before.
