### Integration with your Zendesk account

[Create your chatbot here](https://doko-ai.com/en/sign-up)

<Steps>
    <Step>
        <h3>Go to Settings>Integrations in your chatbot portal and select Zendesk</h3>
       <Frame>
  <img src="/images/zendesk.png" alt="Integrations" />
</Frame>
    </Step>
 <Step>
        <h3>Enter your subdomain and click on Connect</h3>
        <Note>This is your Zendesk account subdomain, for example: <b>https://doko.zendesk.com</b></Note>
       <Frame>
  <img src="/images/dominio-zendesk.png" alt="Connect" />
</Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step>
        <h3>Grant the necessary permissions for Doko to access your Zendesk account</h3>
       <Frame>
  <img src="/images/permisos-zendesk.png" alt="Permissions" />
 
</Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step>
        <h3>Done! Your chatbot can now create tickets in your Zendesk account</h3>
       <Frame>
  <img src="/images/conectado-zendesk.png" alt="Chat Bot" />
</Frame>
    </Step>
</Steps>
