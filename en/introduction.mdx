## What is Doko?

Doko is an advanced chatbot creation platform that combines the power of artificial intelligence with an intuitive and user-friendly interface. Designed for businesses and professionals, Doko allows you to create personalized virtual assistants that can interact with your customers naturally and effectively. And all of this without knowing how to code.

## Main Features

- **Intuitive Creation:** Simple questionnaire to create your chatbot without code, just add your knowledge base.
- **Advanced AI:** Natural language processing for fluid and natural conversations.
- **AI Tools:** Tools for scheduling appointments, saving leads, creating support tickets, and calling an agent.
- **Complete Customization:** Adapt the chatbot to your brand and specific needs.
- **Multichannel Integration:** Connect with WhatsApp, websites, and more.
- **Real-Time Analytics:** Metrics and data to optimize performance.
