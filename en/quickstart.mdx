## How to create your chatbot?
<Steps>
  <Step title="Access your dashboard">
   Click on the 'Create Chatbot' button located in the upper right corner of your dashboard.
   <Frame>
    <img src="/images/dashboard.png" alt="Dashboard" />
   </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the basics">
    Set up the basic aspects of your chatbot, such as the name and knowledge bases.
   <Frame>
    <img src="/images/name-dashboard.png" alt="Name Dashboard" />
   </Frame>
    Add a knowledge base so your chatbot can answer your customers' questions.
    <Note>You can add up to 10 knowledge bases which can be web pages and PDFs.</Note>
    <Frame>
    <img src="/images/kb-dashboard.png" alt="Knowledge Base Dashboard" />
    </Frame>
    <Tip>Make sure your documents contain only text to improve response accuracy.</Tip>
    Here's a tutorial with recommendations

  width="560"
  height="315"

  title="YouTube video player"
  frameborder="0"
  allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture"
  allowfullscreen
></iframe>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Click create">
    Click create and in less than 20 seconds you'll have your chatbot ready to use.
    <Frame>
    <img src="/images/create-button.png" alt="Create Button" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
